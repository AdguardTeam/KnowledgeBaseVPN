## Requisitos del sistema

AdGuard VPN can only be installed on devices with **iOS 15.4 and iPadOS 15.4 or later**.

### If AdGuard VPN has been removed from the App Store

If you dont see AdGuard VPN in the App Store, try changing the country or region in your Apple ID settings. Another option is to create a new Apple account and connect it to the App Store. [Instructions for both cases](/adguard-vpn-for-ios/solving-problems/app-store) are described in a separate article.

After you change the region or link a different account to the App Store, [install AdGuard VPN](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/adguard-vpn-unlimited-fast/id1525373602).
